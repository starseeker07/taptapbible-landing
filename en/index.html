TapTapBible is a Bible transcription app that builds a daily habit of copying Scripture and meditating deeply on the Word. In the middle of a busy day, pause for a moment and engrave the Word in your heart, one character at a time.

How it works

Just three simple steps

1

Pick a verse

Choose the passage you want to meditate on from all 66 books of the Bible, in your preferred translation.

2

Copy it character by character

Type along with the verse on screen. Each correct character fills in; mistakes are highlighted immediately.

3

Watch your records grow

Progress, streaks, and stats build up automatically, turning each small moment of reflection into a lasting daily habit.

What makes TapTapBible special

Copy scripture precisely, one character at a time

Type along with the verse and get real-time feedback on every character. Copying slowly, letter by letter, naturally draws your full attention to the Word.

  • Real-time right/wrong feedback
  • Multiple Bible translations
  • Re-transcribe for deeper reflection

Stay consistent with progress & streaks

See your whole-Bible progress, current streak, total verses copied, and time spent — all at a glance. Each small daily reflection becomes a visible, lasting habit.

  • Whole-Bible progress tracker
  • Daily streak tracking
  • Verse count & time statistics

Keep the verses that move you

Bookmark any verse that touches your heart and add your own personal note alongside it. Come back anytime to read it again and let it sink deeper.

Start without login, sync when you're ready
