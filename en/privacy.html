TapTapBible Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Effective date: June 19, 2026

kukuDev ("Operator") takes the privacy of TapTapBible users seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information is stored on your device and what information is transmitted or processed by the Operator or third-party services in the course of using the Service.

1. Core Principles

  1. The app offers a guest mode that requires no login. Transcription records, bookmarks, and statistics created in guest mode are stored only on your device (locally) and are never transmitted to the Operator's servers.
  2. If you sign in to use account-based features such as backup, sync, or group transcription, that data is transmitted to and stored on the cloud servers (Google Firebase) used by the Operator.
  3. The Operator processes only the minimum information necessary to provide the Service. Some features, such as advertising, operate under the policies of their respective providers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Guest Mode (used without signing in)

  • Transcription records, bookmarks and notes, statistics, streaks, and app settings are stored only in the local database on your device and are never transmitted to the Operator's servers.

2.2. When Signed In

  • Account identification: The unique account identifier (UID) and email address provided when signing in with Google or Apple. When using Apple Sign In, users may choose to hide their email address.
  • Service usage data: Data created by the user — such as transcription records, bookmarks and notes, statistics, and streaks — is linked to the account and stored and synced in the cloud (Firebase).
  • Group transcription data: When creating or joining a group, certain information such as your display name (nickname) and transcription progress may be shared with other members of the same group.

2.3. Advertising and Diagnostic Information

  • Advertising identifier (Ad ID): The app serves ads through Google AdMob, and in doing so the advertising identifier is transmitted to the Google advertising network.
  • Diagnostic and usage information: To ensure stable operation and prevent misuse, information such as device and app version details, crash logs, and App Check tokens may be processed.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • The Operator does not directly collect names, phone numbers, addresses, or similar information through a separate sign-up form (sign-in is handled through Google or Apple authentication providers).
  • The transcription content and records of guest mode users are never transmitted to the Operator's servers.

4. Purposes of Processing

  • Providing the Bible transcription feature along with progress tracking, statistics, and streak records
  • Account-based backup and cross-device synchronization
  • Providing the group transcription feature
  • Serving advertisements and operating the Service
  • Error analysis, security, and prevention of misuse

5. Third-Party Services and Data Processing

The following third-party services are used to provide the Service. Each provider processes information in accordance with its own privacy policy.

6. About Advertising

  • The app is provided free of charge and includes advertisements.
  • The advertising identifier is transmitted to the Google advertising network and may be used and shared for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • Users can reset their advertising identifier or limit personalized ads through their device settings.

7. International Data Transfers

The servers of cloud services used by the Operator, such as Firebase, may be located outside of South Korea. Data synced upon account sign-in may be stored and processed on those servers.

8. Retention and Deletion

  1. Guest mode data is deleted when the user uninstalls the app or clears the app's data on their device.
  2. Data linked to an account is permanently deleted from the servers when the user deletes their account and cannot be recovered.
  3. Advertising and diagnostic information is retained in accordance with the retention policies of each third-party service and the Operator's configuration.
  4. Information required to be retained by applicable law is retained for the period specified by that law and then destroyed.

9. User Rights and How to Exercise Them

  1. Users can manage their own data — including transcription records, bookmarks, and statistics — directly through in-app features.
  2. Users can delete their local data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data on their device.
  3. Signed-in users can request the deletion of their account and all data stored on the servers through the account deletion feature within the app.
  4. Resetting the advertising identifier or limiting personalized ads can be managed through device settings.

10. Children's Privacy

This Service is intended for general users. The Operator does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 14. Parents and guardians may supervise their child's use of the Service, and if it is confirmed that a child's information has been collected, it will be deleted without delay.

11. Security

  • Sensitive configuration values stored locally are protected through the device's secure storage (Keystore/Keychain).
  • Communications with servers are conducted over encrypted channels, and unauthorized access is blocked through measures such as App Check.
  • However, no security measure can guarantee absolute protection.

12. About Bible Text

The copyright of the Bible text provided in the app belongs to the rights holders of each respective translation. The app provides text solely for users' personal meditation and transcription purposes.

13. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be revised in response to changes in law or the Service. In the event of material changes, notice will be provided in advance through the app or this page.
